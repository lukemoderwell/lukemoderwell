Present Values

 UI/UX Design, Art Direction

Present Values is a team of world class consultants who needed a web refresh. They wanted to focus on their team and strong analytical background so we gave them the ability to share their story with case studies. As lead designer on the project, I focused on creating a clean, typographically driven ethic which gave context to the high quality services they offer their clients.

One design challenge was the need to add a more human touch to the website. We decided to focus on the team with high quality, soft-lit photography and a video interview of the CEO, John Handelsman. I was responsible with the art direction of the photography assets.

In addition to creating styleguides, wireframes, polished visual designs, and hi-fidelity prototypes, I also redrew their logo in a vector format to make it more legible and resolution independent.
