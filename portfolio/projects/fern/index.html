Fern Expo

UI/UX Design, Responsive Web

Fern Expo was looking for a new website to spruce up their online presence and better educate cutomers about the services they offer. I was contracted to help lead a design effort to simplifiy, clarify, and help put Fern's best foot forward.

One of the interesting problems during this project was navigation. How would we take a bloated, confusing site architecture and turn it into something usable? After sketching some various options, the client and I decided explore a modular, responsive nav solution that accomodates mobile users and simplified the experience for all devices.

Throughout the Fern project, one thing became very clear: websites can benefit greatly from organizing UI into components and designing systematically. Having reusable and functional modules that can be dropped into new applications means that cliets can gain efficiencies in development and improve time to launch.
